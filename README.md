### <span style="color: rgb(194, 224, 244);">This mod adds a Mermaid species to <a style="color: rgb(194, 224, 244);" href="https://www.curseforge.com/hytale/mods/orbis-origins" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Orbis Origins</a> while using the <a style="color: rgb(194, 224, 244);" href="https://www.curseforge.com/hytale/mods/mermaids" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Mermaids</a> mod as the bases.</span>

## <span style="color: #3598db;"><strong>About <a href="https://www.curseforge.com/hytale/mods/orbis-origins" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Orbis Origins</a></strong>:</span>

![](https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/1437813/description_3667744b-8883-4929-87dc-c47fab7142b3.png)

<span style="color: rgb(194, 224, 244);">Transform your Hytale experience with Orbis Origins, a species selection mod that allows players to choose from 18+ unique playable species, each with distinct attributes, resistances, and visual models.</span>

## <span style="color: rgb(53, 152, 219);">Overview</span>

<span style="color: rgb(194, 224, 244);">Orbis Origins introduces a species system that changes how players approach character creation in Hytale. Upon first joining the server (any world), players receive a Species Selector item that opens a custom GUI, allowing them to browse, preview, and select from a diverse roster of playable species. Each species offers unique gameplay advantages through stat modifiers, damage resistances, and in some cases, starter items.</span>

## <span style="color: #3598db;"><strong>About <a href="https://www.curseforge.com/hytale/mods/mermaids" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Mermaids</a></strong>:</span>

### ![Mermaids-Banner](https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/1430860/description_bdf39dac-b010-48cb-a0c8-663b91de15e0.png)

<span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Ever wanted to be a Mermaid? Now you can with Mermaids, this plugin allows the user to <span style="color: #3598db;"><strong>become a Mermaid</strong></span> in water! Which gives the player increased movement speed, water breathing, night vision, and more while swimming underwater throughout seas, oceans, and rivers.</span>

## <span style="color: rgb(53, 152, 219);">Overview</span>

<span style="color: #c2e0f4;">After entering the water for a couple seconds, you'll turn into a mermaid. Once you enter the land, your legs will regenerate.</span>

*   <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Grow a Mermaid Tail and keep your cosmetics</span>
*   <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Breathe underwater with quicker movement</span>
*   <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Permissions and config</span>
*   <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Configurable transformation option</span>
*   <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Custom model and animations</span>
*   <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">and More!</span>

## <span style="color: #3598db;"><strong>HOW TO INSTALL</strong>:</span>

### <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">First you need to install the <a href="https://www.curseforge.com/hytale/mods/mermaids" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Mermaids</a> mod and <a href="https://www.curseforge.com/hytale/mods/orbis-origins" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Orbis Origins</a> mod.</span>

### <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Client:</span>

<span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Upload the OrbisOrigins-X-Mermaids-#.#.#.jar, OrbisOrigins-#.#.#.jar, and Mermaids-#.#.#.jar files into your %appdata%\Hytale\UserData\Mods\ folder.</span>

### <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Server:</span>

<span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Upload the OrbisOrigins-X-Mermaids-#.#.#.jar, OrbisOrigins-#.#.#.jar, and Mermaids-#.#.#.jar into your servers mods\ folder.</span>

<span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Make sure you delete any previous versions, otherwise your world may not start.</span>
